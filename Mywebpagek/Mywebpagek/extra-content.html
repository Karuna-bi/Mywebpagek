Tips for Aspiring Developers

  1. Start with the basics but don't stay there. Move to real projects as soon as possible.
  2. Read and write code every day; consistency is the key to mastery.
  3. Don't fear failure. Every bug is a lesson learned.
  4. Collaborate and share knowledge with others to grow faster.
  5. Stay curious and keep up with emerging technologies.

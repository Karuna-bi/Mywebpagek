About Gamcheon Village

Known as the "Machu Picchu of Busan," Gamcheon Village is a vibrant and artistic neighborhood located in Busan, South Korea. The village is famous for its colorful houses, narrow alleys, and stunning hillside views overlooking the ocean. Visitors can enjoy local art, quaint cafes, and cultural experiences while exploring this picturesque destination.
